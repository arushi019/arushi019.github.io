About Me

I am a Bengaluru based analyst working in the Tech Division at Goldman Sachs. Previously, I interned at Google and Tessel@Rails Girls Summer of Code program. I am also an AI researcher, specialising in neural architecture search which is kind of like AutoML but for convolutional neural networks. You can check out my publications here. I completed my bachelors in CS from IIIT Delhi in 2020 and have been working since then. I am also a core member of Women in Machine Learning and Data Science (WiMLDS) chapter in Delhi and have a bit of mentoring experience on my hands. I like to paint and watch Netflix during my free time. On some days, I like to travel as well.
